Introduction

I am an initiator, a big-picture thinker, a self-starter, and a motivator. I'm known for my high energy and positive outlook, and I thrive working with people! Having worked closely with students of all different backgrounds and abilities for years, I've fallen in love with the world of higher education. I excel at people-oriented problem-solving - providing practical and personalized solutions to help individuals and teams achieve their goals. I now live in the world of Slate by Technolutions, where I've discovered an incredibly supportive community and a powerful tool to enhance the accessibility of higher education for both students and staff. While I have always loved technology - I'm a bit project management software nerd - I have discovered my niche as a Slate CRM developer. It allows me to combine my love for tech with a deep understanding of the on-the-ground needs of students and educators. In my spare time, I climb, run, mountain bike, cycle, backpack, car camp, hop on cheap flights to random destinations, and generally keep myself entertained.
